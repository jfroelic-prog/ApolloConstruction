New Front Entrance — Exterior (Door 1)

The new front entrance will be located at the current Door 2 area and will become Door 1. Guests will see a raised entrance leading into the secured entry.

The Apollo capsule will move inside the building to guide visitors. Under the raised entrance, lighting will represent different constellations visible in Minnesota.

A large monument sign reading Apollo High School will be clearly visible as guests approach. Construction begins Summer 2027 and will be completed Summer 2028.
