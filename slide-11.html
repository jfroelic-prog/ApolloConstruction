Main Entrance — Interior Capsule View (Door 1)

This is an interior view of our new main entrance at the current Door 2 area, which will now serve as Door 1.

Guests will enter through this new secure entrance and will be buzzed into the main office. The Apollo capsule will be positioned above the entrance as you walk in.

This new entrance will create a welcoming space for students, families, and visitors while strengthening safety and security. This work begins June 2027 and will be complete June 2028.
