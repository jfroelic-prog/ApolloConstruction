Hallways — Fire Walls (400 and 600 Wings)

New fire walls will be added to the corridors in the 400 and 600 wings. These walls will provide added fire protection during an emergency and are required as part of the new construction.

The fire doors feature a wood finish, giving the hallways a fresh and clean look.

Construction begins Summer 2027 and will be complete for the start of the 2027 school year.
