North Resource Center — New Classrooms

This area will be transformed into new classrooms and a new hallway within the existing North Resource Center. The updated space will include classrooms for computer science, culinary, and visual arts, along with a flexible classroom for staff use.

Construction begins December 2026 and will be completed in time for the start of the 2027 school year.
