Fine Arts Renovation

The Fine Arts area will receive a full renovation of the band and choir rooms. The project will also add new storage for theater and fine arts to support student programs.

Lockers in the corridor will be removed to create space for new practice rooms, giving students more room to learn, rehearse, and perform.

Construction begins June 2028 and will be complete before the start of school in September 2028.
