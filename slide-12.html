New Front Entrance — Constellation Lighting (Door 1)

This exterior view shows the new front entrance at the current Door 2 area, becoming Door 1. As guests arrive, they will see a raised entrance leading into the secured entry.

Under the raised entrance, lighting will represent different constellations visible in Minnesota, giving teachers opportunities to connect this feature to classroom learning.

Construction begins Summer 2027 and will be completed Summer 2028.
