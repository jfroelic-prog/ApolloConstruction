Technical Education Center

The new Technical Education Center will expand learning opportunities for students through a Certified Nursing Assistant classroom, an Emergency Medical Technician classroom, an additional flexible classroom, and a makerspace where students can explore and work with new technology.

Construction is already underway and will be completed in time for the start of the 2028 school year.
