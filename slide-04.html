Main Entrance — Front Office (Door 1)

After guests enter through the secure Door 1 entrance, they will be buzzed into the main office. The updated front office will include a new waiting and seating area for visitors.

This space will create a welcoming first stop for families and guests while supporting a secure entry into the building.

This work will begin June 2027 and be complete June 2028.
