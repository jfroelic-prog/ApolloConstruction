Apollo Activities Entrance (Door 7)

The new Apollo Activities entrance will be located at Door 7. This space will create a fresh and welcoming look for one of the main entrances to the building, serving as an inviting entry for families attending evening activities and for students arriving each morning.

The updated entrance will help create a strong first impression and reflect pride in our school.

This work will start Summer 2028 and be completed Fall 2028.
