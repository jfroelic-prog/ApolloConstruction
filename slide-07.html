Apollo Commons

The Apollo Commons will receive a fresh new look with a new ceiling, added wood features on the walls, and new wayfinding to help guide students, families, and guests to the Performing Arts wing. A new concession stand will also be added to better serve events in the area.

Completion will take place at the end of Summer 2027.
