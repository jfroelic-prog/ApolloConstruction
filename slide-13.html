Storm Shelter / 4th Gymnasium

This new space meets current building code requirements for storm shelters, providing a safe, secure area for all students and staff during severe weather events.

During the school day, it will also serve as a fourth gym, giving students more space for physical education and activities. After school, the space will support extracurricular programs and be available for community use.

Construction starts this August and will be complete June 2027.
