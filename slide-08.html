Kitchen & Serving Lines

The Apollo kitchen and serving lines will be renovated to create a larger space and reduce congestion during lunch. The updated design will allow the school to offer more lunch options for students.

Students will return their lunch trays to a new dishwashing area in the center of the commons for easier access and flow.

Construction begins June 2028 and will be complete by the start of the 2028 school year.
