Apex Athletics Center

The Apex Athletics Center will be a multi-purpose indoor athletic complex serving student-athletes from the St. Cloud Crush teams, Apollo High School teams, and St. Cloud Tech High School teams. The facility will also be available for community rentals when not in use by ISD 742 students.

The facility will feature a large indoor turf space supporting year-round training and competition. Sports that will benefit include soccer, softball, baseball, football, lacrosse, golf, and track and field.

Construction begins May 2026 and is expected to be completed Spring 2027.
